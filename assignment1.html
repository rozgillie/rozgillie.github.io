Here is all the documentation for assignment 1. For this assignment I have three blinking LED's, all blinking for different lengths. I used a breadboard and an arduino board to program the lights blinking.

Schematic

These are the schematics for my three LEDs, which were plugged into pins 2, 3, and 4. The red and green LED's have 2200 ohm resistors, while the blue LED has a 100 ohm resistor. Here is the math that went into choosing the resistors: 5V - 1.8V drop for red and green 3.2V 3.2/.02 = 160 -> 220 ohm resistor 5V- 3.2drop for blue 1.8V 1.8/.02 = 90 ohms -> 100 ohm resistor
